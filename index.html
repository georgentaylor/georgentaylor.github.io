Introduction

George Taylor is a postgraduate associate under the Inclusion Economics initiative at the Yale Economic Growth Center. He primarily works to support the evaluation and implementation of the Google Flood Alerts early warning system intervention in Bihar, India. He holds a BSc in Mathematics and Economics from the University of Warwick.
